About Me

As the owner of Kak Lucky Tattoos, and an overall multi-talented artist, I have been tattooing for more than 15 years. I come from a walk-in studio background, tattooing everything from fine line script to large scale custom projects and everything in-between. I enjoy tattooing colour as well as black and grey tattoos, and am always stoked to get involved in any new projects. You can follow me on Facebook and Instagram @garethdoyetattoos
